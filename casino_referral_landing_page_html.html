Reality Check — Play Smart

Remember: casinos are businesses. High playthroughs are common (e.g. 60x on a $20 bonus = $1,200 in wagers). That keeps you on site and often leads to deposits. Don’t accept bonuses blindly — check the terms and research the RTP of the games you play (game providers like Pragmatic or Hacksaw set RTP, not the casino).

  • ✅ Check playthrough before claiming any bonus.
  • ✅ Follow the casino on social for extra promos and surprise drops.
  • ✅ Choose games with higher RTPs and lower variance when chasing bonuses.
  • ✅ Set and respect a daily loss/spend limit.
